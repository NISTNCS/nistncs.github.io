  • Welcome To N.I.S.T. Counselling Service

    NIST COUNSELLING SERVICE, functions under the guidance of Prof. Sangram Mudali and Dr. Rita Bhattacharjee as the Student Counsellor. The Service flagged off in 2014 with a motto to “Nurture, Care and Support” the freshmen coming to a new environment, helping them to deal with the hurdles arising in the starting phase of their life in the institution.

    The Service consists of a team of student guides with well-trained faculty advisors who works for the betterment of the community, providing a healthy atmosphere to the freshmen to evolve socially, mentally, physically and academically.
