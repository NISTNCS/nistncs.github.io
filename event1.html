Outreach program to K.V. Berhampur



Event Date: - 05/07/2017
Written by: Rahul Panda



Time and tide waits for none. Understudies of classes 11th and 12th experience a considerable measure of difficulties in planning for their career. Absence of appropriate guidance and inspiration, the majority of them begin making a few bargains with their aspirations. We a group of 7 Student Guides (Anmol, Parth, Pratyusha, Sarita, Abhijeet, Atul and Rahul) along with our student counselor Dr. Rita Bhattacharjee visited K.V. Berhampur on 5th July for an outreach program.

Returning back to a nearly familiar environment that made us recall our days at school, we broadened our hands to the on edge high schoolers sharing our encounters to life helping them comprehend the difficulties ahead and discover the available career opportunities. It was itself self-rousing listening to these young minds about their plans for life and enlightening them the paths that can help them achieve it. Some wanted to be Engineers, a few Doctors yet never less many of them wanted to rather choose to be lawyers, journalists, event organisers, fashion designers, pilots, and significantly more in their life. To give anything less than your best is to sacrifice the gift in you. It is better to live the dream then to leave it.

It wasn’t just an opportunity to help and counsel our younger brothers and sisters, but was also a chance to re-believe in our motive. It’s time to risen up and turn our dreams to reality, accomplish our goals challenging all hurdles in life and believing in our capabilities. Thanks organisers for believing in us and the all members of our NCS family for being united.
