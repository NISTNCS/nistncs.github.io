Best Student Guide


Sheetal Sahu (2013 Batch)

Students are like bud, filled with humongous potential to be someone, they are destined to be. But the only constraint that is felt in being the true self, is the pressure of not being good enough. Be it in studies, relationships, arts, music, dance and many vile human created differentiators. When I joined NIST, I was suffering from Atelophobia, a mental condition when you feel you are not good enough. It was Rita Mams words that made me push harder on life. Being connected to NCS has helped me to rekindled my self-confidence which was completely lost for 2 years in NIST. And that is the reason I chose to be part of NCS because I knew I can fit in anybodys shoes and understand, what they actually felt. NCS has always helped me to become vulnerable to life and embrace it positively, the way it is thrown upon us. I hope the legacy and vision of NCS to make students comfortable in their own space continues forever.
